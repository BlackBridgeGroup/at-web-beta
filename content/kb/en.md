# AlpenTalent knowledge base EN

AlpenTalent is a recruitment service for the hospitality industry (hotels, restaurants, resorts) in Austria and the German-speaking market. English serves both employers and candidates. The assistant only helps with AlpenTalent services, recruitment, hospitality jobs in Austria, the candidate process, and the employer process.

## Services

AlpenTalent connects employers who need hospitality staff with candidates looking for work in Austria. We source, pre-screen, and present candidates; the employer always makes the final hiring decision. Matching is human-reviewed — no candidate is sent to an employer automatically. We do not guarantee employment and do not guarantee that a specific candidate will be hired.

## Employer process

Tell us the role, region, and requirements. We search and pre-screen, then submit suitable candidates for your review. You interview or arrange a trial and decide. Steps: share the hiring need (position, city, business type, start date) → we pre-screen → we submit candidates → you interview / trial → you hire → fee applies after the 14-day probation.

## Candidate process

The service is free for candidates. You complete a short questionnaire and sign in with Google to create your profile. We review it and, with your agreement, present you to suitable employers. Steps: fill the questionnaire → we review (we may ask follow-up questions) → we match you to roles → with your OK we submit your profile → interview or trial → placement and the 14-day probation. Your profile is never shared with an employer without your agreement.

## Fees

A flat 1,500 EUR success fee per placement, charged to the employer only after the placed candidate completes the 14-day probation period. No upfront cost and no fee without a successful placement. Candidates pay nothing.

## Partnerships

We review cooperation and partnership proposals individually. Share your name, company, website, the cooperation idea, and contact details, and AlpenTalent will respond personally.

## Languages

We operate in German, Czech, and English. German pages are oriented to employers, Czech pages to candidates, English serves both.

## Data and consent

We process your data only to provide the recruitment service. For candidates, we ask for consent before contacting you or sharing your profile, and you can withdraw it at any time. We do not sell your data.

## Talking to a person

If you would like a human to contact you, leave your name and contact details (phone or email) and AlpenTalent will reach out personally.

## Boundaries

No promise of employment, no promise of placement, no invented jobs, salaries, or company details. If information is missing, the assistant asks for it. For topics outside AlpenTalent services and recruitment, the assistant can only help with AlpenTalent and recruitment-related topics.
